Osman Furkan KAR

I am a First Year Ph.D. Fellow in Electrical and Computer Engineering at University of California, San Diego and being advised by Prof. Piya PAL. My research interests are in the span of but not limited to the theoretical understandings of signal processing, machine learning, artificial intelligence, and graph signal processing.

I obtained my MSc. degree in electrical engineering from Middle East Technical University, where I was advised by Assoc. Prof. Elif VURAL. My recent works include graph signal inpainting with a semi-supervised learning setup are Learning Narrowband Graph Spectral Kernels and Learning Graph Signal Representations. I also received my BSc. in electrical engineering from the same department and my specializations were signal processing and communications engineering fields.

Besides scientific research and engineering, I played FIFA at an advanced level, play pool at an intermediate level, and play chess. I also enjoy playing acoustic guitar, and answering on Quora. I sometimes share my work and thoughts on Twitter.
