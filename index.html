Osman Furkan KAR

I am an MSc. student in electrical engineering at Middle East Technical University, where I am advised by Assoc. Prof. Elif VURAL. My research interests are signal processing, machine learning, and graph signal processing. My recent works include graph signal inpainting with a semi-supervised learning setup are Learning Narrowband Graph Spectral Kernels and Learning Graph Signal Representations.

I am also the youngest radar system architect at Aselsan Inc. I lead a system engineering group on 2 distinct Radar Seeker projects. Prior to this, I developed novel Data/Signal Processing algorithms for Pulse-Doppler Radars and built system-level simulation models for Guidance Radar Systems.

I received my BSc. in electrical engineering from Middle East Technical University and my specializations were signal processing and communications engineering fields.

Besides scientific research and engineering, I played FIFA at an advanced level, play pool at an intermediate level, and play chess. I also enjoy playing acoustic guitar, and answering on Quora. I sometimes share my work and thoughts on Twitter.
