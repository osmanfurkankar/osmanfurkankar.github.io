Osman Furkan KAR

I am a MSc. student in electrical engineering at Middle East Technical University, where I am advised by Assoc. Prof. Elif VURAL. My research interests are signal processing, machine learning and graph signal processing. My recent works includes graph signal inpainting with a semi-supervised learning setup are Learning Narrowband Graph Spectral Kernels and Learning Graph Signal Representations.

I am also a full-time radar signal and radar data processing engineer at Aselsan Inc., a top-50 defence company world-wide and #1 in Turkey. At Aselsan, I develop novel Data/Signal Processing algorithms for Pulse-Doppler Radars, build system-level simulation models for Guidance Radar Systems and lead radar-seeker projects as a responsible system engineer.

I received my BSc. in electrical engineering from Middle East Technical University and my specializations were signal processing and communications engineering fields.
